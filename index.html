Prototype Writeup

What have we done so far?

So far, our group has experimented with several possible visualizations for the final project submission in order to better understand the data and pinpoint what we want to focus on. Through collaboration and brainstorming, we decided to create an interactive website that explains the different futures for different countries, based on whether there are high or low emissions. Currently, we have designed a website that incorporates these ideas with a map of the world which allows users to choose certain countries to see the different scenario statistics, and how it is projected to change over time. To strengthen the storytelling aspect, we included a play button that animates how the temperature shifts through color and it clearly demonstrates how high emission scenarios lead to a much warmer world compared to low-emission scenarios.

Challenging Project Tasks

We believe that the most challenging part of our project to design would be thinking of creative and engaging ways to elevate our interactive components, like scrollytelling and introduction animation, as well as making sure there is a clear and compelling storyline throughout. While the technical implementation may not be too difficult, we believe that it would be hard to find ways to communicate to such a broad audience clearly and impactfully. Our plan for the project is to make it easily comprehensible and actionable, so our website would need to be simple enough for the general audience to understand. At the same time, we would need to include evidence that urges people to change and help the growing problem of climate change in the form of global warming. We do not want to overload our audience with information, but to also make sure they are well informed enough about the situation. Our current prototype has the basic idea of what we hope to include in our final submission, but we will have to continue refining and expanding the design to better align with these goals.
